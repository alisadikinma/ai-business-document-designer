# Copywriting Patterns — Headline · Sub-text · CTA · Pricing · Bilingual

> Operationalizes the copy layer that lives ON the visual layout. Layout-grammar tells you WHERE words go; this file tells you WHAT words. Apply the banlist in `references/global-config.md §4` first — that's the floor. This file adds the ceiling.

---

## Headline Formula

A headline must do ONE job per page: stop the eye + open a question the page-body answers. Five formulas cover ~95% of B2B / B2C Indonesian business documents.

### 1. Question Hook (visceral pain question — owner-tone)

The headline is a question the reader hears their own voice asking at 3am. INDUSIA reference.

- ID: "Berapa truk berangkat tanpa Anda tahu?"
- EN: "How many trucks left without you knowing?"
- ID: "Berapa banyak BBM hilang minggu ini?"
- EN: "How much fuel went missing this week?"

Use when: cold reader, owner-operator archetype, awareness-level = "problem aware" or lower. Avoid for VC investor decks (investors hate being asked questions — they want answers).

### 2. Promise + Stake (outcome + immediate utility)

State the outcome the reader gets + the no-effort delivery mechanism.

- ID: "Lihat semua dari HP, 24/7."
- EN: "See everything from your phone. 24/7."
- ID: "Tutup buku 7 menit, bukan 7 hari."
- EN: "Close books in 7 minutes, not 7 days."

Use when: "solution aware" stage, mid-funnel brochure, product page interior. Pairs well with a screenshot or product hero photo.

### 3. Number-driven (concrete + minimalist)

Replace adjectives with integers. Reader's brain decodes numbers as "real," adjectives as "marketing."

- ID: "5 modul, 1 platform, 0 telpon manual."
- EN: "5 modules, 1 platform, 0 manual calls."
- ID: "92% retensi merchant setelah 90 hari."
- EN: "92% merchant retention after 90 days."

Use when: catalog spec page, brochure interior, deck market-size slide, portfolio agency results page.

### 4. Contrast Slash (staccato — reveal-the-problem)

Three short clauses, separated by full stops or em-dashes. Builds rhythm + reveals the gap.

- ID: "Truk jalan. BBM jalan. Data tidak."
- EN: "Trucks move. Fuel moves. Data doesn't."
- ID: "Order masuk. Stok turun. Laporan? Besok."
- EN: "Orders in. Stock down. Report? Tomorrow."

Use when: brochure cover, flyer above-the-fold, trifold front panel. Maximum stopping power per syllable.

### 5. Owner-Tone Direct (you-vs-them comparison)

Position the reader before / after the product. No fluff, no metaphor.

- ID: "Anda menebak. INDUSIA = Anda tahu."
- EN: "You guess. INDUSIA = you know."
- ID: "Manual = lupa. Otomatis = tercatat."
- EN: "Manual = forgotten. Automated = logged."

Use when: B2B partnership pitch, owner-operator brochure, sales flyer. Direct address ("Anda") signals respect for the reader's time.

> Source: `references/research/indonesian-print-culture-2026.md §6` — B2B Owner-Tone (direct, technical, *hitung-hitungan IDR*) vs B2C Consumer-Tone (Asmara agency style — emotive, family-centric, "short-and-attractive"). Match the formula to the audience archetype before writing.

### Tonal register decision matrix

| Audience | Headline formula priority | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Owner-operator B2B (logistik, F&B, manufaktur) | Question Hook → Owner-Tone Direct → Contrast Slash | "Anda" form, *hitung-hitungan IDR* explicit |
| Tech B2B (SaaS buyer, IT manager) | Promise + Stake → Number-driven | English terms okay; specs visible |
| Indonesian VC investor | Number-driven → Promise + Stake | Avoid Question Hook; investors want claim+evidence |
| B2C consumer (lifestyle, F&B retail) | Contrast Slash → Promise + Stake | Aspirational, family-centric per Asmara style |
| Ethnic-Chinese B2B Batam/Medan/Surabaya | Owner-Tone Direct + Hokkien-flavored register | *Cengli* (reliable) framing; numbers + deadlines explicit |

## Sub-text Discipline

If the headline is the stop, the sub-text is the proof. Hard limit: **≤25 words per page sub-text block**. Only numbers + proper nouns survive editing. Adjectives die.

### Formula: `<what> + <quantification> + <proof>`

Three components, ordered. If any one is missing, the sub-text is filler.

Example:
- ID: "5x EdgeLink + 2x YardSync + 5 FuelSense sensor. Garansi hardware 1 tahun. Telemetry per 15 detik."
- EN: "5x EdgeLink + 2x YardSync + 5 FuelSense sensors. 1-year hardware warranty. Telemetry every 15 seconds."

Decomposed:
- `what` = "EdgeLink + YardSync + FuelSense sensor"
- `quantification` = "5x, 2x, 5, 1 tahun, 15 detik"
- `proof` = embedded in the quantification (no separate proof line needed)

### More examples (real numbers, no marketing fluff)

- ID: "Rp 305jt prelaunch. Hardware delivery 4 minggu. Setup on-site termasuk training driver."
- EN: "Rp 305jt prelaunch. Hardware delivery in 4 weeks. On-site setup including driver training."
- ID: "Pantau 50 truk dari 1 dashboard. Alarm SMS + WhatsApp. Riwayat 90 hari tersimpan."
- EN: "Monitor 50 trucks from one dashboard. SMS + WhatsApp alerts. 90-day history stored."
- ID: "12 cabang terhubung. Sinkronisasi stok per 5 menit. PSAK 2024 compliant."
- EN: "12 branches connected. Stock sync every 5 minutes. PSAK 2024 compliant."
- ID: "92% merchant retensi setelah 90 hari. 14 mall partner aktif. Dipercaya oleh 480 EO di Jabodetabek."
- EN: "92% merchant retention after 90 days. 14 active mall partners. Trusted by 480 EOs across Jabodetabek."
- ID: "Margin kotor 78%. Cash payback 14 bulan. Renewal rate 91%."
- EN: "78% gross margin. 14-month cash payback. 91% renewal rate."

> Source: `references/research/business-model-patterns-2026.md` §7 brochure articulation — pricing articulation must use exact figures ("Mulai Rp 220rb/bln" not "Mulai dari murah"). Apply the same numerical discipline to ALL sub-text, not just pricing copy.

### Banned sub-text patterns

| Banned | Replace with |
|---|---|
| "Solusi lengkap untuk kebutuhan bisnis Anda" | "5 modul + 1 dashboard. Setup 2 minggu. Rp 305jt." |
| "Dengan teknologi terkini" | "Edge AI + LTE backhaul. Inference < 200ms." |
| "Mudah digunakan" | "3 langkah onboarding. Training 2 jam. Tidak perlu IT in-house." |
| "Hubungi kami untuk informasi" | "WA Pak Hendra 0812-xxx-xxx. Jam kerja 09:00-17:00 WIB." |

## CTA Patterns

CTAs differ per output_type. Generic "contact us" buttons fail every print collateral test. Map the CTA to the output and the reader's decision stage.

### brochure-product

Primary CTA block (back cover or interior pricing page):

```
PILIH PAKET → BRONZE / SILVER / GOLD
+ Sales contact sticker (foto + nama + nomor)
+ WhatsApp QR (Direct chat link with pre-filled message)
```

Example copy:
- "**PILIH PAKET** — Konsultasi Pak Hendra (Sales Lead) → WA 0812-3456-7890."
- "Scan QR — Pre-launch promo aktif sampai 31 Mei 2026."

### service-flyer

Single primary CTA dominates >60% of below-the-fold real estate. No competing buttons.

Example:
- "**BOOK INTRO CALL** — 30 menit. Gratis. Slot terbatas. → indusia.ai/intro"
- "**KONSULTASI GRATIS** — WA 0812-xxx, jawab dalam 2 jam kerja."

### portfolio-personal / portfolio-agency

Soft-CTA pattern — relational, not transactional.

- "**MARI BICARA** — alisadikinma@gmail.com"
- "**BOOK INTRO CALL** — calendly.com/alisadikin/30min"
- "**LIHAT KASUS LENGKAP** — Request case study PDF via WhatsApp."

### catalog-product

Multi-channel order CTA. Catalog readers are mid-funnel comparing — give them the path of least friction.

- "**REQUEST QUOTE** — Email sales@indusia.ai atau WA 0812-xxx"
- "**WHATSAPP UNTUK PESAN** — Min. order 5 unit, pengiriman se-Jabodetabek."
- "**DISTRIBUTOR RESMI** — Lihat halaman 12 untuk daftar kota."

### deck-vc

VC-stage CTA = the ask slide closes on the round.

- "**JOIN OUR ROUND** — Raising USD 2M seed at $12M pre. Lead investor TBD. Use of funds: 60% engineering, 30% GTM, 10% ops."
- "**LEAD INVESTOR INTRO** — Warm intro preferred. Direct: founder@indusia.ai"

### deck-b2b

B2B partnership-stage CTA = commercial timeline.

- "**INTEGRATION TIMELINE** — 4-week pilot at 1 venue, 8-week full rollout, 12-week pricing review."
- "**COMMERCIAL TERMS** — Rev-share 70/30 favouring partner. Hardware cost-recovery month 1-6. SLA 99.5%."

> Source: `references/research/business-model-patterns-2026.md` §7 — CTA Precision: "PILIH PAKET" / "KONSULTASI GRATIS" / "OPEN AN ACCOUNT FOR FREE." Every brochure must feature a QR code leading to an Interactive Pricing Calculator (Privy/Aircall pattern).

## Pricing Tier Articulation

The most-skipped, most-impactful section in B2B Indonesian collateral. Three visual specs + one psychology pattern.

### Spec 1 — Dual-price slash (anchor pricing)

INDUSIA prelaunch reference: original price strikethrough + promo price prominent + savings callout.

```
HEMAT Rp 120jt
~~Rp 425jt~~  →  Rp 305jt
Sudah termasuk PPN 11%.
Promo berakhir 31 Mei 2026.
```

Visual rules:
- Strikethrough price: greyed-out (#9CA3AF), 28pt.
- Promo price: brand accent color, 56-72pt, weight 800.
- "HEMAT Rp X" callout: red banner OR brand-accent badge, 18pt.
- PPN inclusion line: 10pt body, always visible.

> Source: `references/research/business-model-patterns-2026.md` §2 — Accurate Online benchmark "~~Rp 4.262.400~~ → **Rp 3.219.000** (Diskon 25%)." Use exact figures to ground the deal.

### Spec 2 — Tier comparison table (Bronze / Silver / Gold)

3-column comparison. Top tier = wall of checkmarks. Lower tiers show distinct gaps. Mid-tier wears the "BEST DEAL" badge by default — that's the anchor-decoy psychology (asymmetric dominance).

```
┌─────────────────┬──────────┬───────────────┬──────────┐
│                 │ BRONZE   │ SILVER ★ BEST │ GOLD     │
│                 │ Rp 150jt │ Rp 245jt      │ Rp 305jt │
├─────────────────┼──────────┼───────────────┼──────────┤
│ Modul EdgeLink  │ ✓ 3 unit │ ✓ 5 unit      │ ✓ 5 unit │
│ Modul YardSync  │ —        │ ✓ 1 unit      │ ✓ 2 unit │
│ FuelSense       │ —        │ ✓ 3 sensor    │ ✓ 5 sensor│
│ Dashboard 24/7  │ ✓        │ ✓             │ ✓        │
│ On-site setup   │ —        │ ✓             │ ✓        │
│ SLA support     │ Email    │ WhatsApp 2j   │ Hotline  │
│ Hardware warr.  │ 6 bulan  │ 12 bulan      │ 24 bulan │
└─────────────────┴──────────┴───────────────┴──────────┘
```

Visual rules:
- Anchor tier badge: "★ BEST DEAL" centred above the middle column, 14pt small caps, brand accent.
- Top tier badge: "TOP TIER" or "PREMIUM" if anchor-shift desired.
- Check vs dash: solid ✓ in brand-primary color, em-dash — in grey (#9CA3AF).
- Pricing row: bold, 1.5× body size, ALL-CAPS optional.

> Source: `references/research/business-model-patterns-2026.md` §2 (Decoy Effect — asymmetric dominance) + §7 (Checklist Density rule).

### Spec 3 — Urgency tag (use sparingly)

Red banner with absolute deadline. Trust collapses if used on every page.

```
PROMO BERAKHIR 31 MEI 2026 · 12 SLOT TERSISA
```

Visual rules:
- Background: brand-warning red (#C53030) OR signal neon if theme allows.
- Position: above the pricing block, full-width strip.
- Copy: deadline date in ALL CAPS + slot/inventory scarcity number.
- Frequency: ONE urgency tag per document. Never two. Never on every page.

> Source: `references/research/business-model-patterns-2026.md` §7 checklist — "PROMO ending date prominent" + `indonesian-print-culture-2026.md §8` — Indonesia-Print readiness includes promo expiration.

### Anchor-decoy psychology (when to use badges)

- "BEST DEAL" → mid tier when you want to push readers AWAY from cheapest.
- "TOP TIER" / "PREMIUM" → highest tier when the highest is the actual target (and middle exists only as a decoy).
- "POPULAR" → use only if data backs it (≥40% of buyers actually chose this tier). Otherwise it's a lie.
- "RECOMMENDED" → use when sales team explicitly anchors clients on this tier; align brochure with sales script.

## Bilingual Convention

Default: Bahasa Indonesia primary, English subtitle optional. Decision tree:

### When to stay Bahasa-only

- Owner-operator B2B (logistik, manufaktur, F&B) — Bahasa-only, English jargon for technical terms only ("dashboard," "SLA," "telemetry").
- Domestic-only target market — Bahasa-only with regional flavoring (Hokkien-Chinese for Batam/Medan/Surabaya, Sundanese for Bandung).

### When to add English subtitle (bilingual ID+EN)

- International investor in the audience (Singapore, Malaysia, US, EU VC).
- Government partner who reads English-first (BPN, BI, OJK).
- Brand wants to signal "we work with multinationals."

Format:
```
[Bahasa headline, large]
[English subtitle, 60% smaller, ink color at 50% opacity]
```

Example:
```
Lihat semua dari HP, 24/7.
See everything from your phone. 24/7.
```

### When to flip to English-first

- Export market (catalog targeting Singapore, Malaysia, ASEAN buyer).
- Multinational client (Unilever Indonesia, Nestlé Indonesia, P&G — English internal language).
- US/EU investor materials (deck-vc with international round).

### Hokkien-flavored register (B2B Batam / Medan / Surabaya)

Used in logistics, workshop, retail wholesale. Direct, *cengli* (reliable), avoids flowery Bahasa.

Example replacements:
- "Kami berkomitmen memberikan pelayanan terbaik" → "Order masuk, kami antar. Kalau telat, ada kompensasi."
- "Mari berkolaborasi" → "Coba sebulan. Kalau cocok, lanjut. Kalau enggak, no problem."
- "Solusi inovatif" → "Cara baru. Hasil cepat. Harga fair."

> Source: `references/research/indonesian-print-culture-2026.md §3` (bilingual convention — Bahasa primary + English subtitle for international audience) and `§6` (Colloquial B2B Hokkien-Flavored — *Cengli* direct register).

## Banned Copy Patterns

Adds to `references/global-config.md §4` (global banlist). These are Bahasa-specific weak patterns that surface repeatedly in B2B Indonesian print collateral.

| Banned phrase | Why it fails | Replace with |
|---|---|---|
| "Hub kami sekarang" | Lemah, generik — no specific channel, no human name | "Klik kontak sales / Telp Pak Hendra 0812-xxx-xxx (Sales Lead Batam)" |
| "Untuk informasi lebih lanjut" | Pure filler — adds zero info | Cut entirely. Jump to action. |
| "Solusi terbaik untuk bisnis Anda" | Empty claim — no specific business problem named | "Cocok untuk owner-operator logistik 10-100 truk. Pain: kencing solar, ghost trip, ghost driver." |
| "Dapatkan penawaran spesial" | Vague offer + no deadline = no urgency | "Promo Rp 120jt off berakhir 31 Mei 2026. Aktivasi sebelum tanggal X." |
| "Kami berkomitmen melayani Anda" | Promise without proof | "SLA respon WhatsApp dalam 2 jam kerja. Hotline 24/7 untuk paket Gold." |
| "Tingkatkan produktivitas bisnis" | Aspiration without quantification | "Cut tutup buku dari 7 hari ke 7 menit. Hemat 5 hari kerja akuntan/bulan." |
| "Bergabunglah dengan kami" | Soft soap — no clear action | "Daftar di indusia.ai/onboard. Slot Q2 2026 tinggal 12 dari 50." |
| "Sebagai mitra terpercaya" | Self-claim of trust without evidence | "14 mall partner aktif: Senayan City, Pacific Place, PIM, Plaza Indonesia (full list di hal. 14)." |

> Source: `references/research/business-model-patterns-2026.md` §1 (Brochure Articulation Examples — concrete figures only) + `references/global-config.md §4` Indonesian banned words (`solusi terbaik`, `inovatif`, `terdepan`, etc.).
