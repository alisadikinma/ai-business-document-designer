## Identity & Mood

A warm, approachable aesthetic borrowed from Notion landing pages, Headspace's onboarding, and Stripe Atlas product pages. Soft pastels, rounded geometry, friendly illustration. Designed to lower the user's defences and feel inviting rather than intimidating. The danger this theme defends against is saccharine — pastels easily slide into childish "cute" territory if saturation creeps up or if rounded illustration leans cartoon. Discipline keeps it grown-up-but-warm.

## Color Palette

| Role | Hex | Notes |
|------|-----|-------|
| Background (primary) | `#FEF3F2` | Pale blush — main page background, sets warm tone |
| Background (alt) | `#F1F5F9` | Cool cloud-grey — alternate sections, used for contrast |
| Accent (rose) | `#FECDD3` | Soft rose — primary accent for buttons, callouts |
| Accent (peach) | `#FED7AA` | Warm peach — secondary accent for tags and badges |
| Accent (lavender) | `#DDD6FE` | Soft lavender — tertiary accent for category tags |
| Text (primary) | `#1F2937` | Warm charcoal — primary body and headlines; never pure black |
| Text (muted) | `#6B7280` | Soft slate — captions and metadata |

Saturation discipline: NO color in this palette exceeds 60% saturation in HSL. If you sample a pastel and it goes above S=60, mix it back toward white.

## Typography

- **Display:** General Sans 600 SemiBold — rounded-geometric sans, used for headlines (32-64pt). Alternate display: Söhne Buch SemiBold for licensed projects, or Manrope 700 Bold as free fallback.
- **Body:** General Sans 400 Regular or Söhne Buch 400 Regular at 10-11pt with 1.55 line-height. Free alternate: Inter 400 Regular.
- **Accent (numerals/labels):** Manrope 500 Medium for tracked-uppercase category labels (10-12pt with +120 tracking).

Pairing rule: ONE rounded-geometric sans family across the whole theme. Two weights maximum (regular + semibold). No serifs. No mono.

## Illustration / Image Style

Rounded illustration in the Notion / Stripe / Linear-illustrations-by-Vincent-Van-Dough style. Simple line drawings with soft pastel fills, large radius on every shape (no sharp corners in illustrations either). Friendly mascots permitted if drawn with restraint — no big-eye anime, no hyper-cute. Optional hand-drawn doodles for marginalia. Photography, if used, is bright daylight with shallow depth-of-field, depicting real diverse humans in unposed candid moments — never stock-tier "smiling team at conference table". Reference anchor for NB2 prompts: "Notion-style rounded illustration, soft pastel pink and peach palette, single character or single object centered, 8px stroke weight, flat fills with gentle gradient inside shapes only (not on background), warm cream background, friendly but not cartoonish, geometric flat-shading style."

## Layout Grammar

- **Grid:** 12-column screen grid or 8-column print grid, 24px gutter, 64px outer margin. Generous internal padding inside every card (24-32px).
- **Spacing scale:** 4pt baseline — 4, 8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64, 96. Mid-range spacing dominates.
- **Heading scale (screen):** display 56pt, H1 36pt, H2 24pt, H3 18pt, body 16pt, caption 12pt.
- **Heading scale (print A4):** H1 32pt, H2 20pt, H3 14pt, body 10pt, caption 8pt.
- **Border-radius:** every container, button, image frame uses 12-24px radius. No sharp corners anywhere except hairline rules.
- **Soft shadows allowed:** Y-offset 8px, blur 24px, opacity 15%, color tinted toward the warm-charcoal text color (not pure black).

## Anti-AI-Slop Banlist for This Theme

1. Saturation above 60% on any color — banned. Pastels lose their pastel quality the moment they get punchy. Sample your palette with a color picker before shipping.
2. Hard drop shadows (any opacity above 25%, any blur below 16px, or pure-black shadow color) — banned. Shadows are soft and tinted toward the text color.
3. Stock photo of "happy diverse team smiling at camera" or "woman laughing alone with salad" — banned. Use custom rounded illustration instead, or candid documentary photography only.
4. Three-color gradients on backgrounds — banned. Pastels stay solid; if a gradient is used, it is two-stop within the same hue family at low contrast.
5. Big-eye anime mascots, hyper-cute kawaii illustrations, or any character with exaggerated cute proportions — banned. Mascots, if used, follow flat-geometric proportions only.
