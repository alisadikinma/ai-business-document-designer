## Identity & Mood

Reads like a Hermès printed catalog or an Aman Resorts brochure left on a marble side-table. Heritage typography, deep matte backgrounds, restrained gold accents. The page communicates value through what it does NOT do: no urgency, no claims, no clutter. The viewer is trusted to recognize quality without being told. Mood signals: heritage, patience, exclusivity, craft. The theme fails the moment it tries too hard — gold gradients, sparkle effects, and ornate flourishes all read as costume jewelry.

## Color Palette

| Role | Hex | Notes |
|------|-----|-------|
| Background (primary) | `#0B0B0F` | Deep matte black — main canvas for hero spreads |
| Background (alt) | `#1C1812` | Charcoal-brown — alternate spreads, adds warmth without losing depth |
| Accent (gold) | `#D4AF37` | Rich antique gold — used for hairline rules, monograms, and exactly one focal element per spread |
| Accent (champagne) | `#E8D8B0` | Soft champagne — used for secondary type or muted highlights |
| Text (light) | `#FFFFFF` | Pure white — display headlines and primary body |
| Text (muted) | `#C5C5C5` | Soft grey — captions, metadata, legal copy |

Gold is a precious resource: appears at most twice per spread. Never as a fill, never as a gradient.

## Typography

- **Display (serif):** Didot Bold or Bodoni 72 Bold — high-contrast didone serif at 56-120pt for cover and chapter openers. Both ship with macOS; for licensed cross-platform use, substitute Playfair Display 900 Black.
- **Body (serif):** Garamond Premier Pro 400 Regular or Mrs Eaves OT Roman — 9.5-10.5pt with 1.55 line-height for running copy.
- **Accent (small-caps):** Trajan Pro Regular — used for section labels and monograms in tracked small-caps at 9-11pt with +200 tracking.

Pairing rule: ONE display didone + ONE old-style serif body + Trajan for labels. Three families maximum, all serif. No sans-serif anywhere in this theme. No mono.

## Illustration / Image Style

Commissioned editorial photography only — never stock. Dramatic low-key lighting (chiaroscuro), single product or single subject framed centrally with deep shadow. Gold-foil texture overlays at low opacity for spread separators. Embossed monogram details. Slow-motion video stills if motion is in scope. Product photography on velvet, marble, polished wood, or matte stone surfaces. Reference anchor for NB2 prompts: "Hermès-style editorial product photograph, single leather goods object on matte black marble surface, single directional warm tungsten light from upper-left, deep falloff into shadow, product occupies center-right with 60% negative dark space, no props, no human figures, medium-format film aesthetic, antique gold monogram subtly embossed in lower-right corner, museum-catalog quality."

## Layout Grammar

- **Grid:** 6-column print grid, 40px gutter, 120px outer margin. Most content uses 4 of 6 columns; the remaining 2 stay empty for breathing room. Title pages allow 8-column wide imagery with column-1 reserved for tracked small-caps label.
- **Spacing scale:** 8pt baseline biased to large increments — 16, 32, 48, 64, 96, 128, 192. Cramped spacing is forbidden.
- **Heading scale (print A4):** Cover display 120pt, H1 56pt, H2 28pt, H3 14pt (small-caps), body 10pt, caption 8pt.
- **Heading scale (screen):** display 96pt, H1 48pt, H2 24pt, body 16pt.
- **Rules:** 0.25pt gold hairline only, used as section separators. Never a thick rule. Margins act as the structural device, not borders.

## Anti-AI-Slop Banlist for This Theme

1. Gold gradient backgrounds, gold-foil-everything, or "shimmery" reflection effects — banned. Gold appears as solid `#D4AF37` accent only, never as a gradient, never as a background fill.
2. Sparkle, glitter, lens-flare, or bokeh-particle effects — banned. The theme is matte and grounded, not Las Vegas.
3. Stock "luxury lifestyle" photography (yachts at sunset, couples in tuxedos, champagne glasses clinking) — banned. Use commissioned still-life or architectural photography only.
4. Italic display type set in didone — banned. Didone italics at large sizes read as wedding-invitation kitsch. Use upright didone display only; italics reserved for old-style body serif at small sizes.
5. Centered-justified body copy in long passages — banned. Body always left-aligned with rag-right; centering reserved for short single-line labels under monograms.
