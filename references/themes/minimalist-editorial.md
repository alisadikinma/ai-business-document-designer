## Identity & Mood

Quiet confidence borrowed from the print archive of Kinfolk, Apartamento, and Wallpaper*. Pages breathe. Imagery does the heavy lifting; type recedes. The page feels like a curated gallery wall rather than a sales sheet. Mood signals: considered, slow, expensive-by-restraint. The danger this theme defends against is busy-ness: any element that does not earn its place gets cut.

## Color Palette

| Role | Hex | Notes |
|------|-----|-------|
| Background (primary) | `#FFFFFF` | Pure paper white — main page background |
| Background (secondary) | `#FAF7F2` | Warm cream — used for alternating spreads or sidebar panels |
| Text (primary) | `#1A1A1A` | Near-black — never `#000000`, which reads harsh on cream |
| Text (muted) | `#6B6B6B` | Mid-grey — captions, metadata, photo credits |
| Accent (warm) | `#C8956B` | Aged-bronze warm beige — used at most once per spread for a small detail |
| Hairline rule | `#D4D0C8` | Soft taupe — used for ultra-thin (0.25pt) rules between sections |

## Typography

- **Display (serif):** Playfair Display 400 Regular and Playfair Display 700 Bold — used for cover titles and chapter openers (48-96pt). Alternate display: Cormorant Garamond 500 Medium and Cormorant Garamond 700 Bold for a more bookish feel.
- **Body (sans):** Inter 400 Regular at 9.5-10.5pt with 1.6 line-height. Alternate body: Suisse Int'l Regular if licensed.
- **Accent style:** serif italic from the display family (Playfair Display 400 Italic) used for pull quotes and photo captions. No mono in this theme — replace with small-caps tracking-treated serif.

Pairing rule: ONE serif family + ONE sans-serif family. Maximum two type families on the entire spread. One weight per hierarchy level — H1 is one weight, body is one weight; no stacking three weights of the same family in a heading.

## Illustration / Image Style

High-resolution editorial photography is the dominant visual element. Generous negative space (often 50%+ of the page is whitespace or quiet image). Monochrome or duotone treatment for cohesion. Full-bleed hero photography on cover and section openers. No decorative graphics, no icons, no infographics-as-decoration. If charts are needed, render them as monochrome line drawings on white. Reference anchor for NB2 prompts: "Editorial fashion magazine spread, natural window light on warm matte surface, single product or single subject centered with 60% negative space, muted desaturated palette (creams, taupes, soft warm grey), shot on medium-format film grain, no text overlay, no decorative elements, calm composition, Kinfolk magazine aesthetic."

## Layout Grammar

- **Grid:** 6-column print grid OR 12-column screen grid, 32px gutter, generous 96-120px outer margin. Asymmetric layouts encouraged — content sits in 4 of 6 columns, the other 2 left empty intentionally.
- **Spacing scale:** 8pt baseline — 8, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64, 96, 128, 192. Larger increments dominate; small spacing is rare.
- **Heading scale (print A4):** Cover display 96pt, H1 48pt, H2 28pt, H3 18pt, body 10pt, caption 8pt.
- **Heading scale (screen):** display 72pt, H1 40pt, H2 24pt, body 16pt.
- **Rules:** hairline rules ONLY at 0.25pt or 0.5pt in `#D4D0C8`. Never use a thick rule. Pages built around image-text rhythm: image, breathe, headline, breathe, body, breathe.

## Anti-AI-Slop Banlist for This Theme

1. Drop shadows of any size or opacity — banned globally. Edges meet edges; depth is implied by whitespace, not shadow.
2. Decorative ornaments, dingbats, asterism, fleurons, or "swash" flourishes — banned. The theme is editorial-modern, not Victorian.
3. Multiple type weights stacked within a single heading hierarchy — banned. One weight per level. No "Light + Bold caps combo" effects.
4. Color photography rendered in oversaturated Instagram-tone — banned. All imagery is desaturated by 20-40%, or shot in natural light with film grain, or treated as duotone.
5. Photo collages and montages — banned. One image per spread, generously sized and quiet. Multi-image spreads use a strict 2-up or 3-up grid only.
